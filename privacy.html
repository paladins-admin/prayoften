Privacy Policy

Your trust matters.

Pray Often is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, and how we safeguard it.

Information We Collect

We may collect information you provide directly, including your account details, display name, email address, prayer requests, responses, feedback, and communications with us.


We may also collect limited technical information such as device type, operating system, app version, anonymous analytics, and diagnostic information to improve reliability and performance.



How We Use Your Information

Information is used to provide the Pray Often service, authenticate your account, deliver notifications you have requested, improve application performance, respond to support requests, and maintain platform safety.



Prayer Requests

Prayer requests that you intentionally choose to share with the community may be visible to other users according to chosen privacy settings. Please avoid including confidential or personally sensitive information in public prayer requests.



Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organisational safeguards designed to protect your information against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.



Your Choices

You may update your account information, manage notification preferences, or request deletion of your account in accordance with applicable laws.



Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Material changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.



Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your information is handled, please contact the Pray Often team through the support channels provided within the application.
